ABOUT THE CLUB

Located in the serene coastal area of Chennai, Elite Combat Neelankarai provides world-class training in MMA, Boxing, Kickboxing, and Functional Fitness. Our facility offers spacious training zones, modern equipment, expert trainers, and a motivating atmosphere for both beginners and professional athletes.

WHAT MAKES ELITE COMBAT NEELANKARAI UNIQUE?

  • Spacious Coastal Training Area:Train in a calm, open environment designed for focus, endurance, and skill development.
  • Advanced Training Equipment:Access modern strength, cardio, and conditioning machines for complete fitness growth.
  • Skilled Professional Coaches:Learn from experienced trainers with strong backgrounds in MMA and combat sports.
  • Safe & Supportive Environment:Structured sessions with proper guidance to ensure safety and steady progress.
  • Flexible Batch Timings:Morning and evening classes available for students, professionals, and beginners.
