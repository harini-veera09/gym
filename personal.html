INTRODUCTION:

Our Targeted Personal Training (TPT) program is a premium one-on-one coaching experience designed for individuals who want personalized guidance and faster results. We take the time to understand your current fitness level, lifestyle, challenges, and long-term goals before creating a customized training plan.

ASSESSMENT:

Every client begins with a comprehensive assessment process. Our coaches evaluate mobility, posture, strength, endurance, body composition, and movement patterns to identify strengths and areas for improvement. This allows us to establish a clear baseline and create a program that delivers measurable progress.

PERSONAL TRAINING CUSTOMIZATION:

No two individuals are the same. Your training plan is tailored specifically to your goals, whether that involves weight loss, muscle gain, athletic performance, self-defence preparedness, rehabilitation, or overall fitness improvement. Nutrition guidance and lifestyle recommendations are also integrated into the program for maximum effectiveness.

TRAINING AND MOTIVATION:

Consistency is the key to transformation. Our coaches provide continuous support, accountability, and motivation throughout your journey. Every session is designed to challenge you safely while building confidence, discipline, and sustainable healthy habits that extend beyond the gym.

At Elite Combat, personal training is more than exercise — it's a structured path towards becoming the strongest, healthiest, and most confident version of yourself.
