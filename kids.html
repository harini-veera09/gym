KIDS MMA CLASSES

Safe, Fun, and Skill-Focused Martial Arts Training for Children

Focus: Combat Sports (MMA) training designed specifically for children.

Components: Agility, Coordination, Discipline, Focus, Confidence, Strength, Flexibility, and Balance.

Systems: MMA, Yoga, Calisthenics, and Age-Appropriate Fitness Training.

Why Choose Boxing Classes at Combat Kinetics?

Kids MMA at Elite Combat introduces young athletes to martial arts in a safe, structured, and supportive environment. Designed for children aged 6 to 15 years, the program helps develop confidence, discipline, fitness, and self-defence skills while making learning enjoyable and engaging.

Our experienced instructors understand the unique needs of young students and create a positive atmosphere where every child can learn, grow, and succeed.Kids MMA provides a strong foundation for physical fitness, mental focus, teamwork, and lifelong healthy habits.

WHAT MAKES OUR KIDS MMA PROGRAM SPECIAL?

Our Kids MMA program focuses on developing both physical abilities and important life skills in a fun and supportive environment.

1. Child-Friendly Coaching: Experienced instructors who specialize in teaching and motivating children.

2. Safe Training Environment: Structured classes with age-appropriate drills and safety-focused instruction.

3. Confidence Building: Martial arts training helps children develop self-esteem and positive self-image.

4. Fitness & Coordination: Improve balance, agility, flexibility, and overall physical development.

5. Life Skills Development: Learn discipline, respect, teamwork, perseverance, and goal setting.

Elite Combat helps children become stronger, healthier, and more confident every day.

WHAT YOUR CHILD WILL LEARN

Martial Arts Fundamentals: Learn basic strikes, blocks, movement, and footwork in a safe environment.

Fitness & Agility: Fun activities that improve strength, flexibility, balance, and endurance.

Self-Defence Skills: Practical techniques that promote awareness, confidence, and personal safety.

Teamwork & Respect: Develop positive social skills while training alongside other children.

Discipline & Focus: Build concentration, self-control, perseverance, and goal-setting habits.
